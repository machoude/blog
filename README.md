# DataScouteR

**Football transfer analytics powered by R *

> *Data answers how a player ranks statistically. Scouts answer whether they fit your system.*

---

## What this is

DataScouteR is a Quarto blog that publishes data-driven transfer assessments for football clubs and fans. Every card shows a player's difficulty-adjusted percentile ranking in the target league, a projection tier, per-metric strengths and risks, and an empirical success probability derived from out-of-sample backtesting.

---

## How it works


Percentiles are ranked **within position** (a CM is compared only to other CMs) and based on the player's **most recent season**, not a career average.

---

## Projection tiers

| Tier | Percentile |
|------|-----------|
| Elite / League Star | ≥ 90% |
| Top 5 Caliber | 75 – 89% |
| Reliable Starter | 50 – 74% |
| Rotation / Squad | 25 – 49% |
| Below Average | < 25% |
